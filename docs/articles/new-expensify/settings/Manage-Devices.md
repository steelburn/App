# Manage Devices

You can see which devices are logged into your Expensify account and revoke access for any device you no longer recognize or trust. This is useful if you've lost a device, suspect unauthorized access, or simply want to clean up old sessions.

---

## How to view and revoke device access

1. Click your profile image or icon in the bottom-left corner.
2. Click **Security**.
3. Click **Device management**.
4. Review the list of devices that have access to your account. Each entry shows the device name, operating system, and when it was last active.
5. To remove a device, click **Revoke** next to it.

# FAQ

## What happens when I revoke a device?

The device is immediately signed out of your Expensify account. The user (or you) will need to sign in again on that device to regain access.

## Can I revoke all devices at once?

No, you must revoke each device individually.
